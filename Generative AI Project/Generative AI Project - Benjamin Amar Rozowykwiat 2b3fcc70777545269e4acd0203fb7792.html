Introduction and Use Case

This project aims to leverage the capabilities of generative AI to enhance personal and professional productivity, particularly for individuals with disabilities such as ADHD. The application integrates with everyday technologies like Siri and Notion, using advanced AI models to transform simple voice notes into well-organized, summarized text entries. This system not only helps in capturing fleeting thoughts and ideas swiftly but also ensures they are stored systematically for easy retrieval and reference. By automating the process of note-taking and organization, the tool offers a practical solution for managing information overload in our daily lives, supporting those who benefit from structured environments to enhance focus and efficiency.

The Tools

Siri

Siri is Apple's voice-activated personal assistant, integrated into all its major devices. It uses natural language processing to understand and respond to user requests, from setting reminders to sending messages. In this context, Siri is crucial as it provides an accessible way for users, especially those with disabilities like ADHD, to capture their thoughts verbally without needing to navigate complex interfaces or manually type notes. This hands-free interaction minimizes distractions and aids in focusing and organizing thoughts swiftly.

ChatGPT

ChatGPT is an advanced language model by OpenAI, skilled at generating text that mimics human writing. In the project, it serves two crucial roles to enhance note management. First, it summarizes the content of the notes, distilling detailed information into concise, easily digestible summaries. This feature is useful for quickly grasping the essence of notes without revisiting the entire text. Second, ChatGPT understands the context and topic of each note, which enables it to generate appropriate titles and select relevant tags from a predefined list. This dual functionality not only aids in retaining the core message of the notes but also categorizes them effectively, improving both retrieval and organizational efficiency.

Notion

Notion is the platform you are reading this on! It‚Äôs a versatile workspace application that combines notes, databases, boards, and calendars into one tool. It's highly customizable, allowing users to create systems for personal and professional organization. In the described project, Notion serves as the platform where all processed notes are systematically catalogued. This integration ensures that the information managed through Siri and organized by ChatGPT is easily accessible and usable, providing a centralized and organized digital space ideal for managing tasks and information efficiently.
